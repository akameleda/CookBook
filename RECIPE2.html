Peanut Butter And Jelly

This is a recipe for Peanut butter and jelly (P. B. and J.) - It can be eaten as breakfast or lunch!


Level: Very Easy!

Be sure that you know how to do and use the following things though:

  • Butter Knife
  • Open Jars

You will need these ingredients:

  • Bread - Quantity: 1 piece
  • Jelly - Quantity: Enough to spread over your piece of bread
  • Nut Butter - Quantity: Enough to spread over your piece of bread

You will need these tools:

  • A Butter Knife
  • A Plate
  • Optional: A Toaster

Steps

Optional: Toast your bread in the toaster - Put your bread into the toaster, pull down the knob and wait. Don't leave the toaster unattended. a


1. Lay the piece of bread onto your plate. This should be pretty simple


2. Fill your cup up with water.


3. Put your apples into the cup of water and squeeze the lemons into the cup too.


4. Wait about 30 minutes - 1 hour or until the water becomes peach-ish and smells like fruits.


5. Enjoy!!
