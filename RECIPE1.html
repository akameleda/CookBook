Spa Water - (Apple Lemonade)

This is a recipe for Spa Water - When I was younger, my friends liked to call it Apple Lemonade.


Level: Very Easy!

Be sure that you know how to do and use the following things though:

  • knife
  • Pouring water

You will need these ingredients:

  • Apples - Quantity: 1
  • Water - Quantity: 1 cup - If you want more of this drink, add more water!
  • Lemons - Quantity: Â½ of a lemon

Steps

1. Cut the apple and lemon. Cut them into as many pieces as you like, But not to many pieces. The pieces have to fit in your cup. 2. Fill your cup up with water. 3. Put your apples into the cup of water and squeeze the lemons into the cup too. 4. Wait about 30 minutes - 1 hour or until the water becomes peach-ish and smells like fruits. 5. Enjoy!!


Hope you enjoyed this recipe!!
